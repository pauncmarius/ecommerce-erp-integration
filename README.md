## 🎯 Context

SC NextGen Hardware SRL is a private company based in Iași, operating in the retail of PC hardware and peripherals. Amid the rapid growth of online commerce, the physical store is facing a decline in foot traffic and struggling to keep pace with digital trends.

The proposed solution is to digitize the store through an e-commerce platform connected directly to the company's existing ERP system (already used for accounting and inventory management), eliminating manual order processing and the risk of selling out-of-stock products.

## 🚀 Goal & Objectives

**Goal:** increase the company's competitiveness and sales volume by developing and deploying a proprietary e-commerce platform, integrated bidirectionally and in real time with the existing ERP.

**Strategic objectives (long-term):**
- Increase revenue by at least 15% in the first fiscal year after launch
- Reduce losses from dead stock by 10% over 2 years
- Increase customer retention by 20% in the long run

**Operational objectives (short-term):**
- Fully develop and integrate the platform with the ERP database within 8 months
- Populate the database with specs and images for ~2,000 products
- Train 4 employees (Key Users) to administer the platform

## 🛠 Technical Solution

- E-commerce platform built on an open-source architecture (e.g. **Magento** / **WooCommerce**), hosted on a dedicated server
- Custom **API connector** for real-time bidirectional sync with the ERP (stock, invoicing, orders)
- Automated flow: online order → ERP stock check → product reservation → invoice generation → logistics notification
- EV SSL certificate, dedicated hosting (1 year), backup on 4TB external HDD
- Hybrid **ROPO** / **Click & Collect** business model
